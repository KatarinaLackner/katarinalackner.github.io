# Angi — Interactive prototype

A clickable prototype that ties together 5 screens from the Angi service-selection flow.

## Flow

1. **What kind of cleaning do you need?** — Choose cleaning type, then **Next** or **Back**.
2. **What kind of location is this?** — Home/Business, then **Next** or **Back**.
3. **Building your project** — Loading screen; advances automatically after 3 seconds or when you tap **Click Here**.
4. **We have matching pros in your area!** — Contact form; tap **View Matching Pros** to continue.
5. **One more step...** — Pro selection; tap **Request quotes** to finish.

**Close (X)** on any screen returns to step 1.

## How to use

Open `index.html` in a browser. Use the dots at the bottom to jump to any step, or use the on-screen **Next** / **Back** and primary CTAs to move through the flow.
